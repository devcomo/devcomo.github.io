Next Meetup: July 27th

Jul 18th, 2011

The time is upon us and Wednesday July 27th is the next comorichweb meetup. This month we’ll be meeting up at our usual Carfax location and we have a few short talks lined up. To start things off Roger Webb will be giving a presentation on Symfony2. After that James Carr (that’s me!) will share some of his experiences developing a web application using serverside coffeescript with examples. Finally, there’s some time for open discussion and lightning talks as both talks are scheduled to be short in length… I know Clint has had tons of good tips and tricks on git to share with us amongst other things. :) 

As always, we have a few items up for raffle from our sponsors.

  • A JetBrains License good for any of thier products
  • Two O’Reilly Books (HTML5 and Javascript Design Patterns)
  • E Book coupons

Also, I’ve come into a ton of Atlassian starter licenses that are due to expire, so all attendees who want one will get one. See you there! ;)
