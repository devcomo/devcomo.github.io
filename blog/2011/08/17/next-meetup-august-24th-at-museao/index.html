Next Meetup: August 24th at Museao!

Aug 17th, 2011

August is quickly coming to a close which means that the next CoMoRichweb meetup is quickly approaching. Our next meetup is August 24th and this month (as well as for the foreseeable future) we’ll be meeting at the Museao building off of Buttonwood Drive. Doors open at 5:30pm to socialize with drinks and refreshments provided and we’ll begin at 6pm. 

This month we’ll be covering deployments so come on our and learn how to convert your day long, error prone, manual deployment process into a one click release button. Greg Aker and Justin Voss will be presenting on Fabric and Robert Boyd will be presenting on Chef. 

As always we’ll have a few raffle items to give out, from IntelliJ licenses, ebook coupons, and more! Just remember to tweet something with the hashtag #comorichweb to be eligible. 

See you there!
