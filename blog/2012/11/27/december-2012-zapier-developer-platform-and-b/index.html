December 2012: Zapier Developer Platform and Book'd

Nov 27th, 2012

ComoRichWeb December 12th Meetup

You won’t want to miss our great speakers this month at ComoRichWeb! They are founders of two different companies started in Columbia, and will be speaking about technology and product development behind their ventures.

December’s meetup is on Wednesday, December 12th! It will be hosted at Reynolds Journalism Institute (directions). Come by around 5:30pm - the talks start at 6. We meet in the glass room right in the main lobby. Come hungry if you want, as we will definitely be getting some good food, compliments of Zapier.

Intro to Zapier Developer Platform

Mike Knoop

Zapier is a web app to connect other online services together. We support over 120 services. More than half were added by third parties via our Developer Platform. I will demo the Platform and add a brand new app live on stage.

Mike Knoop is a co-founder at Zapier and recent Mechanical Engineering grad from Mizzou. Originally from Wildwood, Missouri, he is working full time at Y-Combinator alum, Zapier, hacking on Backbone, Coffeescript, and Sass. In past lives he has been seen as a Facebook Forum Moderator, writer for InsideFacebook.com, and Z80 assembly programmer for the TI-83/84 calculator series.

Lesson Learned from Building Book’d

Jamie Stephens

Book’d allows small businesses to list their services and availability and begin taking online bookings from customers in minutes. It is built with Ruby on Rails. Jamie will share some of lessons he has learned over the last few years about product development and running Book’d.

Jamie Stephens is a software engineer who spent the last dozen or so years helping other businesses get their ventures off the ground before deciding to venture out onto his own with Book’d. He spends most of his time working with Ruby on Rails, but also tries to design things when no one is looking.

Sponsors

Zapier will be sponsoring food for this meetup, and Mizzou Advantage is sponsoring the venue. Be sure to thank them for supporting our group!

Zapier

Zapier makes it easy to connect hundreds of the most popular web services, automate boring tasks and get stuff done. It was started in Columbia, MO at Startup Weekend. Check them out at zapier.com or say hi to @zapier.

Mizzou Advantage

Mizzou Advantage fosters interdisciplinary collaboration among faculty, staff, students and external partners to address and solve real-world needs and problems at the University of Missouri.

See you in December!
