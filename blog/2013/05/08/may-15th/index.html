The May meet up is on Wednesday, May 15th, hosted at Reynolds Journalism Institute. The talks start at 6:00. We meet in the glass room right in the main lobby.

Web Scraping with Python and Scrapy — for Great Justice!

Ceili Cornelison

Web scraping can save infinite hours of valuable human-time. I will be giving an overview of Scrapy, an open source web crawling and scraping framework built for Python, and demonstrating how you, too, can script time-consuming but unavoidable web tasks out of your daily workflow.
Ceili Cornelison (@cornelisonc) spends her days developing web applications for Delta Systems Group in Columbia, MO. She works primarily in PHP/HTML and CSS/JavaScript, and spends her nights contemplating open source development, spanish reds, emerging web technologies and the future of all mankind.
