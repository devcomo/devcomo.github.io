About

devcomo is a monthly user group Columbia, Mo that is focused on development and design. Simply put, if it is interesting then it’ll be discussed!

Contacting the Group

The best way to get ahold of all group members and keep up to date with the news is on the ComoRichWeb mailing list. Also, if you follow @devcomo on twitter you can see updates and contact the group.

Some of our talks and projects are available on the ComoRichWeb GitHub organization, and you can find many of the members on GitHub.

Meeting Time And Location

We meet every third Wednesday of the month at Reynolds Journalism Institute on Mizzou campus and meetings start around 6pm unless otherwise specified.

We’re always looking for speakers! If you’d be interested in speaking feel free to mention it at a meetup, post to the Google + group , or send james.r.carr@gmail.com a message and we’ll put you on the schedule.

Getting There

Reynolds Journalism Institute is located on Mizzou campus, off of ninth street. Close parking is available at the Hitt Street Garage. Walk in the front door, and we are straight back in the big glass room.
